# LegacyMap AI Support

LegacyMap AI is an informational cemetery mapping, genealogy support, memorial preservation, and historical record digitization app.

## Getting Help

For app support, use the GitHub Issues page:

https://github.com/lanray07/LegacyMap-AI/issues

Please include:

- Your device model and iOS version
- The app version
- A short description of the issue
- Screenshots only if they do not reveal private family or memorial details

## Important Disclaimer

LegacyMap AI is an informational historical tool only. OCR may contain inaccuracies, AI outputs require review, genealogy links may require independent verification, and the app is not legal identity verification or official archival certification.

## Data Deletion

LegacyMap AI stores app data locally on the device using SwiftData. You can delete app data from the app settings or by deleting the app from your device.
