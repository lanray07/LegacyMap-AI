# LegacyMap AI Terms Of Use

LegacyMap AI provides cemetery mapping, genealogy support, memorial preservation, volunteer coordination, OCR placeholders, and historical record digitization tools for informational and historical purposes.

## Informational Use Only

LegacyMap AI does not provide guaranteed ancestry accuracy, legal identity verification, official archival certification, legal advice, or professional restoration advice.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for reviewing OCR results, checking AI-generated summaries, verifying genealogy relationships, following cemetery rules, and getting appropriate permission before cleaning, repairing, photographing, or publishing memorial information.

## Subscriptions

Premium and Heritage Pro subscription features are managed through Apple StoreKit. Subscription pricing and availability may vary by region.

## Respectful Use

Users should handle memorial records, grave photos, family notes, and cemetery history respectfully and lawfully.
