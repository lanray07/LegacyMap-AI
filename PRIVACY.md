# LegacyMap AI Privacy Policy

LegacyMap AI is designed as an offline-friendly historical preservation app. The app stores cemetery, memorial, photo, volunteer, and record data locally on your device using SwiftData.

## Data You May Add

You may choose to add:

- Memorial names and dates
- Cemetery and plot notes
- Headstone or record photos
- Digitized historical text
- Family relationship notes
- Volunteer restoration notes

## Permissions

LegacyMap AI may request location access for cemetery mapping and navigation, photo library access for uploads, camera access for scanning, microphone access for voice input, and speech recognition access for dictation.

## AI And OCR

Mock AI is enabled by default in the app. If a future remote AI backend is connected, selected text or images may be sent to the configured backend endpoint only to provide the requested OCR, summary, or historical insight feature. API keys should never be stored inside the app.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. LegacyMap AI does not process payment card details directly.

## Limitations

LegacyMap AI is an informational historical tool only. OCR may contain inaccuracies, genealogy links may require independent verification, and AI summaries must not be treated as verified legal identity records or official archival certification.

## Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored app data from the app settings or by deleting the app from your device.
